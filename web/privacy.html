Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

1. What data we collect

We do not use tracking cookies, Google Analytics, or third-party marketing trackers. When you visit this website, our servers automatically record standard, anonymized connection logs. This includes your IP address, browser type, referring page, and timestamp.

2. How we use this data

This data is used strictly for security monitoring, preventing abuse, and understanding basic, aggregate website traffic (e.g., counting how many total visitors we get).

3. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your server log data with any third parties or advertisers. However, in the event that this website is involved in a bankruptcy, merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be sold or transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Third-Party Services

Map Rendering

We use Mapbox to display our interactive orbital maps. When you view the map, your browser connects directly to Mapbox servers to retrieve the map tiles and rendering code. During this process, Mapbox may collect certain technical information, such as your IP address, device information, and anonymous usage data. For more information on how Mapbox handles this data, please review the Mapbox Privacy Policy.
